People

I'd say I'm generally a fan of people. There are some people I like, and others I don't. Overall, though, people are pretty alright.

My Dog, Buddy

I have a very cute and loyal dog named Buddy! He's my favorite animal, probably. I couldn't remember how to add attachments to emails so I used a stock photo instead. He's a somewhat similar breed, though!

Boats

I'm starting to run out of ideas for things I like, but I think I like boats. I've only ridden in one three times and I got really sea sick the second time, but I still had fun the other two times. That's 66% of the time! Seeing as how I, on average, have enjoyed boats I think it's safe to say they're one of the things I like.

Food

I, like many people I've met, eat food on a fairly regular basis. While there are some times that it doesn't taste great, other times I really like it! It's honestly one of my defining characteristics.

Water

In addition to food, I also drink water quite often. I've found that, when it comes to people, there's a spectrum of water enjoyment. On one side there are people who are crazy for water and drink it all the time, and on the other are people who almost never do! On that spectrum, I'd say I'm somewhere in the middle.

Music

I love listening to music on my commute to the office! It's fun to hum and, if I'm feeling spontaneous, sing along to! My tastes can often get a little bit out there. Some of my favorites include "Yellow Submarine" by The Beatles, "Bohemian Rhapsody" by Queen, and Beethoven's 4th symphony.
